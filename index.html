Reimagining affordable housing with vision, equity, and impact.

Our Mission

The Aztlan Organization is a BIPOC-led real estate development firm focused on building deeply affordable housing throughout California. We blend catalytic capital with community vision to create sustainable, long-term housing solutions.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every working family, veteran, and unhoused person has access to dignified, lasting housing—free from red tape and built on justice.

Our Core Values

Equity. Urgency. Accountability.
We don't wait for change—we build it. Our commitment to affordability, sustainability, and community voice drives every decision.

What Makes Us Different

While many developers rely on slow-moving tax credits, we innovate with flexible capital, community-first design, and long-term ownership—proving deeply affordable housing can be done without compromise.

Our Dual-Track Model

The Aztlan Organization operates with a dual-track strategy. While we are early in our journey and do not yet have completed projects to showcase, we are actively building a portfolio across both government-backed and non-governmental affordable housing. This dual approach allows us to remain nimble, scale faster, and deliver housing for families, veterans, and underserved communities.

Camino del Sol

Camino del Sol is a 96-unit affordable housing project located in San Ysidro, designed for families and veterans. The site includes both a supportive services center integrated within the housing and a separate Center of Anthropology, offering cultural and educational programming for the surrounding community.

National City Project

An urban infill redevelopment vision that blends retail with 100% affordable housing, designed for long-term impact in the South Bay.
